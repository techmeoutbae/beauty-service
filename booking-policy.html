BOOKING POLICY

Policies designed to protect premium appointment windows.

This page keeps the operational details clear while still matching the same polished visual system as the rest of the site.

Deposits

  • Deposits are applied to the final service balance unless the offer states otherwise.
  • Premium or longer appointment windows may require higher deposits to reserve time.
  • Consultation-first services may need provider approval before full booking.

Cancellations & No-Shows

  • Late cancellations may result in a forfeited deposit based on the final studio policy.
  • No-show appointments should be outlined clearly to protect staff time and reserved windows.
  • Rescheduling options should be defined alongside any applicable notice period.

Arrival & Eligibility

  • Late arrival language should explain how appointment time may be adjusted.
  • Medical, consent, or candidacy requirements should be surfaced for advanced services.
  • Age restrictions, patch tests, and preparation requirements should remain clearly visible where relevant.
